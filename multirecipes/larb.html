Somtum

Description

This dish is from Thailand, Also known as Thai Spicy pork salad

Ingredients (One serving)

  • Minced pork 100 gram
  • Sliced pork liver 50 gram
  • Parsley (as you like)
  • Finely chopped shallot 3 tbsp
  • Fish sauce 2 tbsp
  • Lime Juice 2 tbsp
  • Sugar 1/2 tsp
  • Chilli Flakes (as you like)
  • Roasted rice 2 tbsp
  • Thai mint (for garnishing)

Steps

  1. Boil your minced pork and your pork liver to just finished
  2. add your shallot parsley and all your seasoning in the pot
  3. keep mixing until finished
  4. Garnish with Thai mint
  5. Enjoy !!

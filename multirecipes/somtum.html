Somtum

Description

This dish is from Thailand, Another name is Thai Green Papaya Salad, also known as Som Tum or Som Tam.

Ingredients (One serving)

  • Green Papaya (unriped) 1
  • Garlic 5 cloves
  • Chilli Peppers 5 pods
  • Cheery tomatoes 2 diced
  • Roasted peanuts 2 tbsp
  • Long beans 1 pod
  • Fish sauce 2 tbsp
  • Dried small shrimps 2 tbsp
  • Tamarind paste 1/4 cup
  • Coconut sugar 1 tbsp

Steps

  1. Slice your green papaya to thin stripes.
  2. Put your chilli in mortar and pastel and smah it.
  3. Add garlic, tomatoes, peanut,long beans,dried shrimps and smash all of them together.
  4. Add your sliced green papaya and keep smashing
  5. Add all the seasoning including fish sauce, tamarind paste and coconut sugar
  6. Smash all the ingredients together until combined
  7. Enjoy !!
